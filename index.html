Privacy Policy

Overview

ViPLink is a VPN application developed by WalkerTech256. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The honest answer is: none. But we want to be specific about that so you can verify it yourself.

Information We Collect

ViPLink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required — you never provide a name, email, or any personal detail
  • No usage logs — we don't record what sites or services you access
  • No IP address logging — your real or tunneled IP is never stored
  • No device identifiers — no advertising ID, device ID, or hardware info is collected
  • No analytics SDK — there is no third-party analytics framework in the app
  • No crash reporting — crash data is not sent to any external server

Permissions and Why We Need Them

ViPLink requests certain Android permissions to function. Here is what each one is for:

INTERNET The VPN tunnel routes your traffic over the network. This permission is required for any network activity.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE &
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE Android requires these for any app that runs a persistent background service. The VPN and tether services must stay active while in use.
BIND_VPN_SERVICE This is Android's core VPN permission. It allows the app to intercept and tunnel your device's network traffic through the encrypted connection.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Used to monitor your connection status so the app can respond gracefully to network changes like switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Android's battery optimizer can kill background services unexpectedly. This permission lets ViPLink request an exemption so the VPN connection doesn't drop silently.
WAKE_LOCK Keeps the CPU from sleeping while the VPN tunnel is maintaining an active connection.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Used to show a persistent notification while the VPN is running. Android requires this for foreground services, and it lets you see at a glance that your VPN is active.
VIBRATE Used for optional notification feedback on supported devices.

VPN Traffic

When ViPLink is connected, your internet traffic passes through the VPN tunnel. The app processes this traffic locally on your device to route it correctly. No browsing history, traffic content, DNS queries, or connection metadata is logged, stored, or sent anywhere.

Tethering

ViPLink includes a feature that allows you to share your active VPN connection with other nearby devices. This feature is entirely user-initiated. No information about connected devices or their traffic is logged or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

ViPLink is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you have a concern about a child using this app, please reach out using the contact details below.
